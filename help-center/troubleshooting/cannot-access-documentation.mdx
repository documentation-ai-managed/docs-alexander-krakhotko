## Symptoms

- Page does not load
- "404 Not Found" error

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check URL">
    ````
    Confirm that the documentation URL is correct:
    https://docs.alexanderkrakhotko.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Browser Cache">
    ````
    Clear your browser cache and reload the page.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If issues continue, email support@alexanderkrakhotko.com
  </Step>
</Steps>
