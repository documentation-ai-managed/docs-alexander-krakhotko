## Quick Tutorial

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In">
    ````
    Go to https://docs.alexanderkrakhotko.com and log in with your credentials.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Project">
    ````
    Click "New Project" on your dashboard and enter your project name.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation Pages">
    ````
    Select your project and click "Add Page" to start writing your docs.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Team Members">
    ````
    Use the "Invite" button to add collaborators by email.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
