## Get Started in Minutes

Follow these steps to set up your first documentation space.

<Steps>

<Step title="Create Your Space" icon="plus">

  Sign up at `https://alexanderkrakhotko.com` and click "New Documentation Space." Name it for your project, like "MyApp Docs."

</Step>

<Step title="Add Your First Page" icon="file-text">

  Use the editor to write in Markdown or MDX. Here's a simple example:

  <CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,MDX">

  ````markdown
  # Project Overview

  This documents your app.

  ## Features
  - Fast setup
  - Scalable
  ````

  ````jsx
  ---
  title: Project Overview
  ---

  ## Features

  <Callout kind="info">This is dynamic content.</Callout>
  ````

  </CodeGroup>

</Step>

<Step title="Customize and Share" icon="settings">

  Apply your brand color `#3B82F6` in theme settings. Publish and share the live URL with your team.

</Step>

</Steps>
